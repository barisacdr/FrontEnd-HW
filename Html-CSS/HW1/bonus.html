TV Shows

Mr. Robot

Elliot, a brilliant but highly unstable young cyber-security engineer and vigilante hacker, becomes a key figure in a complex game of global dominance when he and his shadowy allies try to take down the corrupt corporation he works for.

Doctor Who

The further adventures in time and space of the alien adventurer known as the Doctor and their companions from planet Earth.

Westworld

At the intersection of the near future and the reimagined past, waits a world in which every human appetite can be indulged without consequence.

The Mandalorian

The travels of a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy, far from the authority of the New Republic.

documentaries

Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey

An exploration of our discovery of the laws of nature and coordinates in space and time.

Planet Earth

Emmy Award-winning, 11 episodes, five years in the making, the most expensive nature documentary series ever commissioned by the BBC, and the first to be filmed in high definition.

Making a Murderer

Filmed over a 10-year period, Steven Avery, a DNA exoneree who, while in the midst of exposing corruption in local law enforcement, finds himself the prime suspect in a grisly new crime.

The Social Dilemma

Explores the dangerous human impact of social networking, with tech experts sounding the alarm on their own creations.

Bowling for Columbine

Filmmaker Michael Moore explores the roots of America's predilection for gun violence.

Animes and Animation Series

Grave of the Fireflies

A young boy and his little sister struggle to survive in Japan during World War II.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

In a war-torn world of elemental magic, a young boy reawakens to undertake a dangerous mystic quest to fulfill his destiny as the Avatar, and bring peace to the world.

Steins;Gate

After discovering time travel, a university student and his colleagues must use their knowledge of it to stop an evil organization and their diabolical plans.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Violet Evergarden

In the aftermath of a great war, Violet Evergarden, a young female ex-soldier, gets a job at a writers' agency and goes on assignments to create letters that can connect people.

Code Geass

After being given a mysterious power to control others, an outcast prince becomes the masked leader of the rebellion against an all powerful empire
